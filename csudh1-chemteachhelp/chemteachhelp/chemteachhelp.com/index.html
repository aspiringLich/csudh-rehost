Important Issues
  • The pages will run properly with Netscape 4.x and Internet Explorer 4.x or higher. Javascript must be enabled. In most instances, failure of the page to execute is the result of a browser incompatibility.
  • Your screen should be set to a minmum resolution of 800x600. The pages will execute at lower resolutions, but for some of them, parts of the text will overlap.
  • To navigate:
    • First,place your mouse over one of the topics on the left of the screen
    • Second, click on the desired page from the list presented. Be certain that both the page# and title match the assignment.
    • Please report problems using the link below or the links available on any of the pages. Try to include as much detail as possible, if you report a problem. As a minimum include the page and the browser being used.
