Converting Masses

This page provides drill and practice in converting mass measures between the English (pounds and ounces) and the metric (grams, etc). It will help you with both your math skills and you understanding of both systems of measure.

Usage

  • To begin-press "New Problem" and values will appear in three of the cells.
  • Put the appropriate numerical value in the empty cell and press "Check Answer."
  • The results will appear in the second table, along with your running score.
  • If your answer is incorrect, you should redo and resubmit it.
