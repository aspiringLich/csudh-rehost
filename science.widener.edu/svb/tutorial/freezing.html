Freezing and Boiling Points of Aqueous Solutions

This page is an exercise in colligative properties. When you press "New Problem" a solute formula appears in the first cell and two of the other cells will show values. Determine the value for the missing cells and press "Check Answer." Results appear in the second table.

Hints

  • Be careful about dissociating solutes
  • All solutions are treated as ideal
  • It is assumed there are not additional processes beyond the possible solute dissociation
  • You can should continue to work on a problem until you get it correct, but you can get a new problem at any time.
